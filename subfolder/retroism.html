Retroism

The nostalgic fancination for retro culture often found in vaporwave

Retroism to many could seem like a complicated idea. Basically it boils down to a distinctive practice in imitating fashion, design, or culture from the past.
This fascination with the retro is what attracts many producors and consumers of Vaporwave. Their combined nostalgia for the past and lethargic loathe of modern society combine to ignite a wildfire of imagination.
The uniqueness of Vaporwave is only matched by it's queer allure of such a small subset of music consumers.
